Our Story

For nearly three decades, World Trust Express has been connecting businesses across the globe with reliable, efficient logistics solutions tailored to their unique needs.

Global Logistics Excellence Since 1995

Founded in 1995 and headquartered in Belgium, World Trust Express has grown from a regional transport provider into a truly global logistics partner. With nearly three decades of experience, we have developed the infrastructure, expertise, and international network needed to move cargo of all kinds — from small parcels to oversized industrial equipment — safely and on time.

Today, we proudly serve clients across Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Our success is built on strong partnerships with leading carriers, freight forwarders, and port authorities worldwide. This global presence enables us to offer flexible, cost-efficient shipping routes by air, sea, road, and rail, while maintaining the personalized service of a dedicated logistics team.

Our Comprehensive Services

We specialize in freight forwarding, heavy cargo, and project logistics — the complex shipments that demand careful planning and execution.

Air Freight

Our air freight services ensure time-sensitive cargo reaches its destination quickly and securely. With partnerships with major airlines worldwide, we offer competitive rates and reliable scheduling for urgent shipments.

Ocean Freight

For larger shipments requiring cost-effective transportation, our ocean freight solutions provide reliable container and bulk shipping options with comprehensive tracking and customs clearance support.

Land Transportation

Our road and rail services provide seamless connectivity for regional distribution. With a fleet of modern vehicles and experienced drivers, we ensure safe and timely delivery across continents.

Heavy Cargo & Project Logistics

Specializing in oversized and heavy equipment transport, we handle complex project logistics with precision planning, specialized equipment, and route surveys to ensure safe delivery.

Warehousing & Distribution

Our strategically located warehouses offer secure storage with inventory management, cross-docking, and just-in-time distribution services to optimize your supply chain.

Customs Brokerage

Our experts navigate complex international regulations to ensure smooth customs clearance, documentation compliance, and duty optimization for all your shipments.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a global logistics partner, our story is one of growth, innovation, and commitment to excellence.

1995

Foundation

World Trust Express was established in Belgium with a focus on regional road transportation services, serving local businesses with reliable delivery solutions.

2002

European Expansion

We expanded our operations across Europe, establishing offices in key logistics hubs and developing partnerships with carriers throughout the continent.

2008

Global Reach

Entered intercontinental markets with the launch of our air and ocean freight divisions, connecting Europe with Asia and the Americas.

2015

Technology Integration

Implemented advanced tracking systems and digital platforms, providing clients with real-time visibility and streamlined logistics management.

2020

Pandemic Response

Adapted our operations to maintain supply chain continuity during global challenges, implementing safety protocols and alternative routing solutions.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our green logistics program, focusing on carbon footprint reduction, alternative fuels, and environmentally responsible practices.
